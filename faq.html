What does Van Dijk mean?

Van Dijk (Dutch pronunciation: [vɑn ˈdɛik] ( listen)) is a Dutch toponymic surname meaning "from (the) dike". With 56,441 people, it was the fifth most common name in the Netherlands in 2007. Abroad, people with this surname usually abandoned the ij digraph, resulting in names like Van Dyke and Van Dyk.
