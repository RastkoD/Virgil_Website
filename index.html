Ballon d'Or Nominee

Liverpool top
 the shortlist by far!

Players from six different leagues and twelve different clubs can hold up hope of the coveted Ballon d’Or trophee as French outlet France Football disclosed the 30 nominees who pretend to the throne.

Liverpool FC is represented by the far most appointed players.

Trent Alexander-Arnold, Alisson, Roberto Firmino, Sadio Mané, Mohamed Salah, Virgil van Dijk, Georginio Wijnaldum.

Record winners Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi are candidates just as Ajax Amsterdams former and actual stars Matthijs de Ligt (Juventus), Frenkie de Jong (Barcelona), Dusan Tadic and Donny van de Beek. Real Madrids Luka Modric, who carried off The Best FIFA Men's Player award in 2018, however is not on the list.
